How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  2. Factor orientación sur + insulation bueno: 50 BTU/m³
  3. Calculate BTU: 65m³ × 50 = 3250 BTU/h
  4. To m² method fast: 25m² × 600 BTU/m² = 15000 BTU/h
