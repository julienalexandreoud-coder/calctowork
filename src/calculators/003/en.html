Building foundations? Enter your footing dimensions and quantity to calculate total concrete volume. Includes cement bags, blinding layer advice, and waste factor for accurate ordering.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculate volume unitario: 1.5m × 1.5m × 0.5m = 1.125 m³ per footing
  2. Calculate volume total: 1.125m³ × 4 = 4.5m³ of concrete
  3. Calculate cement: 4.5m³ × 7 = 31.5 → 32 bags
  4. Calculate steel of reinforcement: 4.5m³ × 120 kg/m³ = 540 kg
  5. Calculate formwork: perimeter × height × quantity = 2(1.5+0.5)×0.5×4 = 8m²

How It Works

This calculator uses standard formulas to compute accurate results based on your inputs. Enter your values and click Calculate to get instant results.

Input Fields

  • Footing length (m)
  • Footing width (m)
  • Footing depth (m)
  • Number of footings

Result Outputs

  • Total concrete volume (m³)
  • Excavation volume (m³)
  • Cement bags (50 kg)
  • Sand (m³)

Formula

Volume per footing = Length x Width x Depth; Total Volume = Volume per footing x Quantity
