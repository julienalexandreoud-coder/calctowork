Building concrete stairs? Enter total rise, run and step width to get number of steps, tread dimensions, riser height and total concrete volume for the entire staircase.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Volume per step: 0.30 m × 0.18 m × 1.2 m = 0.065 m³
  2. Total for 10 steps: 0.065 m³ × 10 = 0.65 m³
  3. Add 10% waste for formwork: 0.65 m³ × 1.10 = 0.72 m³
  4. Cement: 0.72 m³ × 7 bags/m³ = 5 bags

How It Works

This calculator uses standard formulas to compute accurate results based on your inputs. Enter your values and click Calculate to get instant results.

Input Fields

  • Step tread (m)
  • Step riser (m)
  • Step width (m)
  • Number of steps

Result Outputs

  • Concrete volume (m³)
  • Cement bags (50 kg)
  • Sand (m³)
  • Gravel (m³)

Formula

Volume per step = tread × riser × width; Total = volume × steps × (1 + waste%)
