How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculer metres de tuyau: 80m² × (÷ 15) = 80 × 6.67 = 533.33m
  2. Appliquer facteur 1.par bordes: 533m × 1.10 = 586m de tubo
  3. Calculer circuitos: 586m ÷ 100m/circuito = 5.86 → circuitos
  4. Colector necessaire: salidas (vías)
