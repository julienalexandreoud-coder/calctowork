How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Berechnette 7!
  2. Multiply: 7 × 6 × 5 × 4 × 3 × 2 × 1
  3. Step by step: 7×6=42, 42×5=210, 210×4=840
  4. Continue: 840×3=2520, 2520×2=5040, 5040×1=5040
