How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Given: P=202650 Pa, V=0.015 m³, n=1.2 mol, T=0 (unknown)
  2. Rearrange: T = PV ÷ (nR)
  3. Calcolate: T = (202650 × 0.015) ÷ (1.2 × 8.314)
  4. T = 3039.75 ÷ 9.9768 ≈ 304.68 K
