How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculezte surface: 8.75 × 4.25 = 37.m²
  2. Calculezte perimeter: 2 × (8.75 + 4.25) = m
  3. Calculezte diagonal: √(8.75² + 4.25²) = √94.8125 = 9.m
  4. Results: 37.m² surface, m perimeter, 9.m diagonal
