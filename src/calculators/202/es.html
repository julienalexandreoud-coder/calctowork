How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Largo
  • Ancho

Result Outputs

  • Área
  • Perímetro
  • Diagonal

Formula

Área = Largo × Ancho | Perimeter = 2 × (Largo + Ancho) | Diagonal = √(Largo² + Ancho²)
