How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculer volume unitario: 0.30m × 0.30m × 3m = 0.27m³ par poteau
  2. Calculer volume Total: 0.27m³ × 6 = 1.62m³
  3. Calculer ciment: 1.62m³ × 7 = 11.34 → sacs
  4. Calculer acier: 1.62m³ × kg/m³ = kg
  5. Calculer coffrage: caras × 0.30m × 3m × 6 = 21.6m²
