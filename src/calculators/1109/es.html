How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Backsplash area: 3 m × 0.6 m = 1.8 m²
  2. 10 cm tile area: 0.10 × 0.10 = 0.01 m² per tile
  3. Tiles needed: ceil(1.8 ÷ 0.01 × 1.1) = 198 tiles
  4. Typical box = 25 tiles → 8 boxes

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Largo de la pared
  • Alto de la pared
  • Tamaño del azulejo
  • Huecos de ventanas/puertas

Result Outputs

  • Área de la pared
  • Azulejos necesarios
  • Cajas necesarias
  • Desperdicio

Formula

Area = (Wall length × height) - window openings; Tiles = ceil(area ÷ tile_size² × 1.1); Boxes = ceil(tiles ÷ per_box)
