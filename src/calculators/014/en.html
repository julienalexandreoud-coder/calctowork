How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculate area: 10m × 2.6m = 26m²
  2. Calculate placas (doble): 26m² × 2 caras ÷ 2.88m² × 1.1 (waste) = 20 placas
  3. Calculate perfiles canal (floor/techo): 10m × 2 × 1.1 = 22 units
  4. Calculate montantes (each 0.60m): (10m ÷ 0.60m + 1) × 2 = 34 montantes
  5. Calculate tornillos: 26m² × 20 ud/m² = 520 tornillos
