How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculer surface: 10m × 2.6m = 26m²
  2. Calculer placas (doble): 26m² × caras ÷ 2.88m² × 1.1 (perte) = placas
  3. Calculer perfiles canal (sol/techo): 10m × 2 × 1.1 = unites
  4. Calculer montantes (chaque 0.60m): (10m ÷ 0.60m + 1) × 2 = montantes
  5. Calculer tornillos: 26m² × ud/m² = tornillos
