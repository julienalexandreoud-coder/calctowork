How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculatete area: 6m × 4m = 24m²
  2. Calculatete volume: 24m² × 0.20m = 4.8m³ of concrete
  3. Calculatete cement: 4.8m³ × 7 = 33.6 → 34 bags
  4. Calculatete steel: 24m² × 12 kg/m² = 288 kg
  5. Plan formwork for slab edges and openings

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Slab length (m)
  • Slab width (m)
  • Slab thickness (m)
  • Steel (kg/m²)

Result Outputs

  • Slab area (m²)
  • Concrete volume (m³)
  • Cement bags (50 kg)
  • Steel weight (kg)

Formula

Area = Length x Width; Volume = Area x Thickness; Steel = Area x kg/m²
