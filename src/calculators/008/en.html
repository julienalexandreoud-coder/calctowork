Calculate concrete volume and steel for solid reinforced concrete slabs. Free construction calculator for foundations and floor slabs.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculate area: 6m × 4m = 24m²
  2. Calculate volume: 24m² × 0.20m = 4.8m³ of concrete
  3. Calculate cement: 4.8m³ × 7 = 33.6 → 34 bags
  4. Calculate steel: 24m² × 12 kg/m² = 288 kg
  5. Plan formwork for slab edges and openings

How It Works

This calculator uses standard formulas to compute accurate results based on your inputs. Enter your values and click Calculate to get instant results.

Input Fields

  • Slab length (m)
  • Slab width (m)
  • Slab thickness (m)
  • Steel (kg/m²)

Result Outputs

  • Slab area (m²)
  • Concrete volume (m³)
  • Cement bags (50 kg)
  • Steel weight (kg)

Formula

Area = Length x Width; Volume = Area x Thickness; Steel = Area x kg/m²
