How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcular área: 6m × 4m = 24m²
  2. Calcular volume: 24m² × 0.20m = 4.8m³ de concreto
  3. Calcular cimento: 4.8m³ × 7 = 33.6 → 34 sacos
  4. Calcular aço: 24m² × 12 kg/m² = 288 kg
  5. Calcular fôrma lateral para bordas e aberturas
