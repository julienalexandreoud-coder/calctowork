How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcolate contribution margin per unit
  2. Divide fixed costs by contribution margin
  3. Round up to whole units
  4. Calcolate break-even revenue

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Costi fissi (€)
  • Prezzo di vendita (€)
  • Costo variabile unitario (€)

Result Outputs

  • Unità break-even
  • Vendite break-even (€)
  • Margine di contribuzione

Formula

Break-even Units = Fixed Costs ÷ (Price - Variable Cost)
