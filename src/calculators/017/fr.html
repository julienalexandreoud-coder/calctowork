How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Convertir epaisseur: 3cm = 0.03m
  2. Calculer volume de mortier: 40m² × 0.03m = 1.2m³
  3. Calculer ciment: 1.2m³ × kg/m³ = kg (8.sacs)
  4. Calculer sable: 1.2m³ × 1.1 = 1.32m³ de sable
  5. Appliquer perte du 5% si est necessaire
