How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcular espessura média: (0.4m + 0.3m) ÷ 2 = 0.35m
  2. Calcular volume do muro: 10m × 2m × 0.35m = 7m³
  3. Calcular cimento: 7m³ × 7 = 49 sacos
  4. Calcular aço: 7m³ × 90 kg/m³ = 630 kg
  5. Calcular cofragem (duplo cara): 10m × 2m × 2 = 40m²
