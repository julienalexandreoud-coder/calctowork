How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculate thickness average: (0.4m + 0.3m) ÷ 2 = 0.35m
  2. Calculate volume of the wall: 10m × 2m × 0.35m = 7m³
  3. Calculate cement: 7m³ × 7 = 49 bags
  4. Calculate steel: 7m³ × 90 kg/m³ = 630 kg
  5. Calculate formwork (doble cara): 10m × 2m × 2 = 40m²
