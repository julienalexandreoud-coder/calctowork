How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcular volumen: 90m² × 2.5m = 225m³
  2. Calcular caudal: 225m³ × 0.5 ren/h = 112.5 m³/h
  3. Convertir a L/s: 112.5 ÷ 3.6 = 31.25 L/s
  4. Seleccionar ventilador: 120-150 m³/h (con margen)
