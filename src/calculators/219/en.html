How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Apply Euclidean algorithm: 60 ÷ 48 = 1 remainder 12
  2. Continue: 48 ÷ 12 = 4 remainder 0
  3. GCD = 12 (last non-zero remainder)
  4. LCM = (48 × 60) ÷ 12 = 240
