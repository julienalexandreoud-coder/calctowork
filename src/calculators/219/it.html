Calcola GCD via Euclidean algorithm; LCM = (a×b)/GCD(a,b). Esempio: GCD(48, 18) = GCD = 6; LCM = 144. Calcolatore gratuito e preciso, senza registrazione.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Apply Euclidean algorithm: 60 ÷ 48 = 1 remainder 12
  2. Continue: 48 ÷ 12 = 4 remainder 0
  3. GCD = 12 (last non-zero remainder)
  4. LCM = (48 × 60) ÷ 12 = 240
