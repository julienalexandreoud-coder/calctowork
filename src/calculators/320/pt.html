How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculate total growth ratio
  2. Calculate nth root (n=years)
  3. Subtract 1 and convert to percentage

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Valor inicial
  • Valor final
  • Anos

Result Outputs

  • CAGR %

Formula

CAGR = (Ending Value ÷ Beginning Value)^(1/n) - 1
