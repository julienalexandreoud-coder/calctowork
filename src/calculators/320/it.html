How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcolate totale growth ratio
  2. Calcolate nth root (n=years)
  3. Subtract 1 and convert to percentage

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Valore finale

Result Outputs

Formula

CAGR = (Ending Value ÷ Beginning Value)^(1/n) - 1
