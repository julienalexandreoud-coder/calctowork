How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Measure your weight in kilogramos (ej: 70 kg)
  2. Measure your height in centímetros and converts a meters (ej: 175 cm = 1.75 m)
  3. Square the height to the square (1.75² = 3.0625)
  4. Divide the weight between the height to the square (70 ÷ 3.0625 = 22.8)
  5. Compare with ranges: <18.5 low weight, 18.5-25 standard, 25-30 sobrepeso, >30 obesidad
