How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Convertir watts a kilowatts
  2. Multiplicar por horas de uso
  3. Multiplicar por tarifa

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Potência
  • Horas
  • Tarifa/kWh

Result Outputs

  • Energia kWh
  • Custo

Formula

Energía (kWh) = Potencia (W) × Horas / 1000; Costo = Energía × Tarifa
