How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Identify the costo and the price of sale
  2. Calculate the gain: price - costo
  3. Calculate the margin: (gain / price) × 100
  4. Calculate the markup: (gain / costo) × 100
  5. Interpret the results
