How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Identify what you want to calculate with the Fence Picket Calculator.
  2. Enter the fence length in the input field.
  3. Enter the picket width in the input field.
  4. Enter the gap between pickets in the input field.
  5. Fill in the remaining fields with your measurements.
  6. Click Calculate to see your result instantly.
