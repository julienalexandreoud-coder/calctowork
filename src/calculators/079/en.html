How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Add all the costs fijos mensuales: rental of nave/oficina, insurance, teléfono, software, gestoría
  2. Add the costs variables: fuel, allowances, consumibles, repairs of tools
  3. Calculate the hours facturabl is current is to the month (descarta hours of administración, budgets, desplazamientos not facturables)
  4. Divide the cost total monthly between the hours facturabl is to Obtain the cost hour current
  5. Añade the margin of profit deseado (20-30% to autónomos, 15-20% to empresas consolidadas)
  6. Apply the IVA (21%) if vend is a client final; not apply IVA if invoic is a empresa (inversión of the sujeto liability)
