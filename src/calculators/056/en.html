How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculate demand of heating: 120 m² × 50 W/m² = 6000 W = 6.0 kW
  2. Calculate demand of ACS: 4 people × 0.2 kW = 0.8 kW
  3. Add demands: 6.0 + 0.8 = 6.8 kW thermal total
  4. Apply COP estimated (aerotermia ≈ 3.5): 6.8 ÷ 3.5 = 1.94 kW electrical
  5. Select pump of heat commercial of 7-8 kW thermal
