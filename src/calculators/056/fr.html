How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculer demanda de calefacción: m² × 50 W/m² = 6000 W = 6.kW
  2. Calculer demanda de ACS: personas × 0.kW = 0.kW
  3. Additionner demandas: 6.0 + 0.8 = 6.kW térmicos totales
  4. Appliquer COP estimado (aerotermia ≈ 3.5): 6.÷ 3.5 = 1.kW eléctricos
  5. Selectionner bomba de chaleur comercial de 7-kW térmicos

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Surface habitation
  • Niveau d'isolation
  • Personnes (ECS)

Result Outputs

  • kW thermique total
  • kW électrique consommé
  • COP estimé

Formula

P_térmica = surface × Coef(W/m²) ÷ 1000 + Personas × 0.2 | P_eléctrica = P_térmica ÷ COP
