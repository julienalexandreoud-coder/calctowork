O que é um date difference calculator for?

A date difference calculator precisely calculates the time elapsed between two dates: in exact days, full weeks, approximate months, and years. It's an essential tool in labor law (calculating seniority or severance), medicine (weeks of pregnancy, treatment duration), logistics (delivery deadlines), and personal planning (countdowns).

Como funciona o cálculo

The calculator counts the calendar days between both dates and converts them:

  • Exact days: direct count including leap years.
  • Weeks: days ÷ 7 (full weeks plus remaining days).
  • Months: full calendar months plus remaining days.
  • Years: full years plus remaining months and days.

Step-by-step example

How many days have you worked at your company if you started September 1, 2020?

  1. Start date: 09/01/2020
  2. End date: today, 04/25/2026
  3. Difference: 2,062 days (accounting for leap year 2024)
  4. Equivalent to: 294 weeks and 4 days, or 67 months and 24 days, or 5 years and 7 months

Most common uses

  • Labor law: Calculate exact seniority for severance pay, settlement, and service bonuses.
  • Pregnancy: Determine weeks of gestation from the last menstrual period (LMP) and estimated due date.
  • Legal and contractual deadlines: Verify if warranty periods, statutes of limitation, or contract expiration dates have elapsed.
  • Event planning: Exact countdown for weddings, trips, exams, or project deadlines.
  • Treatment tracking: Monitor how many days you've been following a medication, diet, or training regimen.

Leap years and date differences

A leap year has 366 days instead of 365. A year is a leap year if it's divisible by 4, except century years (divisible by 100), unless they're also divisible by 400. This affects calculations when the date range includes February of a leap year. Our calculator automatically accounts for all leap years in the selected range.
