How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculer surface du forjado: 8m × 6m = 48m²
  2. Calculer nombre de viguetas: 6m ÷ 0.70m + 1 = 9.57 → viguetas
  3. Calculer bovedillas: 48m² ÷ (0.70m × 0.25m) ≈ unites
  4. Calculer volume capa de compresión: 48m² × 0.04m = 1.92m³ (aprox)
  5. Calculer ciment pour capa: 1.92m³ × 7 ≈ sacs
