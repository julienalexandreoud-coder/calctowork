How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcolate IRPF tax deduction
  2. Calcolate social security deduction
  3. Subtract deductions from gross
  4. Divide by 12 for monthly net

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Stipendio lordo annuale (€)
  • IRPEF (%)
  • Contributi previdenziali (%)

Result Outputs

  • Netto annuale
  • Netto mensile
  • IRPEF
  • Contributi

Formula

Net Salary = Gross - (Gross × IRPF%) - (Gross × SS%)
