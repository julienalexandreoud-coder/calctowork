How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculer carga base: 3.kW × 0.kg/kW = 0.kg
  2. Calculer tramo avec carga extra: m - m = m
  3. Calculer carga adicional R32: m × 0.kg/m = 0.kg
  4. Additionner cargas: 0.140 + 0.100 = 0.kg Total
  5. Verifier subenfriamiento (8-12 K) et recalentamiento (5-8 K) tras La carga
