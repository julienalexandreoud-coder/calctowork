How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcolare carga base: 3.5 kW × 0.04 kg/kW = 0.140 kg
  2. Calcolare tramo con carga extra: 10 m - 5 m = 5 m
  3. Calcolare carga adicional R32: 5 m × 0.02 kg/m = 0.100 kg
  4. Sommare cargas: 0.140 + 0.100 = 0.240 kg totale
  5. Verificare subenfriamiento (8-12 K) e recalentamiento (5-8 K) tras la carga

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Unit power
  • Gas type

Result Outputs

  • Base unit charge

  • Total gas charge

Formula

Carga_total = Base(0.04 kg/kW × P) + Extra((L-5) × fattore). R32: 0.02 kg/m, R410A: 0.025 kg/m
