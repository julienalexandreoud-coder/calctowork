How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculatete carga base: 3.5 kW × 0.04 kg/kW = 0.140 kg
  2. Calculatete tramo with carga extra: 10 m - 5 m = 5 m
  3. Calculatete carga additional R32: 5 m × 0.02 kg/m = 0.100 kg
  4. Add cargas: 0.140 + 0.100 = 0.240 kg total
  5. Verify subenfriamiento (8-12 K) and recalentamiento (5-8 K) tras the carga
