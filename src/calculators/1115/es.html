How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Wall area: 6 m × 2.7 m = 16.2 m²
  2. Standard sheet (1.2 × 2.4 m) = 2.88 m²
  3. Sheets with 8% waste: ceil(16.2 ÷ 2.88 × 1.08) = 7 sheets
  4. Joint compound: ~0.5 kg/m² = 8 kg; Tape: ~1.5 m/m² = 24 m

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Largo de la pared
  • Alto de la pared
  • Tamaño de la plancha
  • Número de capas

Result Outputs

  • Área de la pared
  • Planchas necesarias
  • Pasta de juntas
  • Cinta necesaria

Formula

Area = Length × Height × layers; Sheets = ceil(area ÷ sheet_size × 1.08)
