How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Source frequency f₀ = 500 Hz, v_sound = m/s
  2. Source velocity vₛ = m/s (toward observer, positive)
  3. Observer velocity vₒ = m/s (toward source, positive)
  4. Apply formule: 500 × (343 + 10) ÷ (343 - 25) = 500 × ÷ 318
