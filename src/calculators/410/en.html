How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Enter weight, height and edad
  2. Apply formula Mifflin-St Jeor to hombre and mujer
  3. Multiply BMR per 1.2 (sedentario) or 1.55 (asset) to TDEE
  4. Compare results between géneros

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Weight (kg)
  • Height (cm)
  • Age

Result Outputs

  • BMR (male)
  • BMR (female)
  • TDEE sedentary (male)
  • TDEE sedentary (female)

Formula

Hombre: BMR = 10 × weight + 6.25 × height - 5 × edad + 5; Mujer: BMR = 10 × weight + 6.25 × height - 5 × edad - 161
