How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Flat roof area: 12 m × 8 m = 96 m²
  2. Adjust for 30° pitch: 96 m² ÷ cos(30°) = 110.9 m² effective
  3. Add 10% waste for hips and valleys: 110.9 m² × 1.10 = 122 m²
  4. Bundles (3 m² per bundle): 122 m² ÷ 3 = 41 bundles

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Longueur du toit
  • Largeur du toit
  • Pente du toit
  • Bardeaux par botte

Result Outputs

  • Surface du toit
  • Carrés de toiture
  • Bottes nécessaires
  • Facteur de perte

Formula

Area = L × W; Effective = area ÷ cos(pitch); Bundles = effective × (1 + waste%) ÷ coverage
