How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Ingresa poids corporal en kg
  2. Sélectionnez METs de la actividad (1=reposo, 5=caminar rapide)
  3. Ingresa duración en minutes
  4. Aplica: METs × 3.5 × poids × minutes ÷ 200

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Poids
  • METs
  • Minutes

Result Outputs

Formula

Calories = METs × 3.5 × poids (kg) × minutes ÷ 200
