How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Convertire portata: 200 m³/h ÷ 3600 = 0.0556 m³/s
  2. Calcolare sezione libre: 0.0556 ÷ 2.0 = 0.0278 m²
  3. Determinare lado quadrato: √(0.0278) × 100 = 16.7 cm
  4. Normalizar a misure comerciales: 20×20 cm
  5. Estimar alcance del chorro: √(0.0278) × 10 = 1.7 m
