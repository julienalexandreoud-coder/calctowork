How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  3. Aplica formula específica per género

Formula

Hombre: LBM = 0.407 × peso + 0.267 × altezza - 19.2; Mujer: LBM = 0.252 × peso + 0.473 × altezza - 48.3
