How to calculate a tip

The tip calculator helps you figure out how much to leave as a gratuity at a restaurant, café, or any service where tipping is customary. It also splits the total automatically when dining in groups.

Tip formula

Tip = Bill amount × (tip % ÷ 100)

Total = Bill + Tip

Per person = Total ÷ Number of people

Recommended tip percentages

  • 10%: Basic or below-average service.
  • 15%: Good service, standard in many countries.
  • 20%: Excellent service, standard in the US and Canada.
  • 25%+: Exceptional service or to recognize special effort.

Practical example

$85 bill for 4 people, 20% tip:

  1. Tip: $85 × 0.20 = $17
  2. Total: $85 + $17 = $102
  3. Per person: $102 ÷ 4 = $25.50/person
