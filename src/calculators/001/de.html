How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Multiplizieren: 5m × 3m = 15m² Fläche
  2. Multiplizieren: 15m² × 0.20m = 3m³ Betonvolumen
  3. Materialien berechnen: 3m³ × 7 Säcke/m³ = 21 Säcke Zement
  4. Sand berechnen: 3m³ × 0.65 = 1.95m³ Sand
  5. Kies berechnen: 3m³ × 0.90 = 2.70m³ Kies

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Länge
  • Breite
  • Dicke

Result Outputs

  • Betonvolumen
  • Zementsäcke (50 kg)
  • Sand
  • Kies

Formula

Volumen = Länge × Breite × Dicke
