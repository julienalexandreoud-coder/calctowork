Ordering concrete for structural beams? Enter beam width, depth, length and quantity to calculate volume, cement bags, steel weight and formwork area.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculate volume per beam: 0.25m × 0.50m × 5m = 0.625m³
  2. Calculate total volume: 0.625m³ × 4 = 2.5m³
  3. Calculate cement: 2.5m³ × 7 = 17.5 → 18 bags
  4. Calculate steel: 2.5m³ × 140 kg/m³ = 350 kg
  5. Calculate formwork: 2(0.25+0.50)×5×4 = 30m² (excludes bottom if bearing on wall)

How It Works

Formula

Volume = Length x Width x Depth; Steel = Volume x Density
