How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcular volume por viga: 0.25m × 0.50m × 5m = 0.625m³
  2. Calcular volume total: 0.625m³ × 4 = 2.5m³
  3. Calcular cimento: 2.5m³ × 7 = 17.5 → 18 sacos
  4. Calcular aço: 2.5m³ × 140 kg/m³ = 350 kg
  5. Calcular fôrma: 2(0.25+0.50)×5×4 = 30m²
