How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculate volume: 8m × 4m × 1.5m = 48m³ = 48000 L
  2. Filtrar 2 veces per day: 48m³ × 2 = 96m³/day
  3. Calculate flow rate: 96m³ ÷ 24h = 4m³/h
  4. Select bomba: 4-5 m³/h (margin of seguridad)
