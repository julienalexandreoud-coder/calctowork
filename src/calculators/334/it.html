How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcolare pagamento massimo permitido (ingreso × ratio debito)
  2. Convertire tasso anual a mensual
  3. Calcolare numero totale de pagos mensuales
  4. Applicare formula de valore presente de anualidad

How It Works
