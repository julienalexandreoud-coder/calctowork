How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Area: 6 m × 4 m = 24 m²
  2. Depth in metres: 8 cm ÷ 100 = 0.08 m
  3. Volume: 24 × 0.08 = 1.92 m³
  4. Crushed granite density ≈ 1.6 t/m³: 1.92 × 1.6 = 3.07 tonnes
