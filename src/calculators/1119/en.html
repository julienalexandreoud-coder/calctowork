Planning a rock garden or driveway base? Enter area, depth and rock type to get weight in tonnes and cubic yards for decorative rock, gravel or crushed stone.

How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Coverage area: 4 m × 3 m = 12 m²
  2. Convert depth: 5 cm = 0.05 m
  3. Volume needed: 12 m² × 0.05 m = 0.6 m³
  4. Weight (gravel density 1.6 t/m³): 0.6 m³ × 1.6 = 0.96 tonnes

How It Works

This calculator uses standard formulas to compute accurate results based on your inputs. Enter your values and click Calculate to get instant results.

Input Fields

  • Area length (m)
  • Area width (m)
  • Rock depth (cm)
  • Rock density (t/m³)

Result Outputs

  • Coverage area (m²)
  • Rock volume (m³)
  • Weight (tonnes)
  • Estimated cost

Formula

Area = L × W; Volume = area × depth; Weight = volume × density
