How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Coverage area: 4 m × 3 m = 12 m²
  2. Convert depth: 5 cm = 0.05 m
  3. Volume needed: 12 m² × 0.05 m = 0.6 m³
  4. Weight (gravel density 1.6 t/m³): 0.6 m³ × 1.6 = 0.96 tonnes

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Lunghezza area
  • Larghezza area
  • Profondità ghiaia
  • Tipo di roccia

Result Outputs

  • Area coperta
  • Volume necessario
  • Tonnellate necessarie
  • Iarde cubiche

Formula

Area = L × W; Volume = area × depth; Weight = volume × density
