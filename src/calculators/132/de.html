How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Initial P₀ = 150, rate r = 3.5% = 0.035, time t = 8 years
  2. Berechnette exponent: 0.035 × 8 = 0.28
  3. Berechnette e^0.28 ≈ 1.3231
  4. Final value: 150 × 1.3231 ≈ 198.4702
