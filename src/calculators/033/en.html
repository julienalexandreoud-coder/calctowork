How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Convert height to pressure: 15m = 1.5 bar (10m = 1 bar)
  2. Calculate friction losses: 15m x 0.15 = 2.25m additional head
  3. Total pressure: 15m + 2.25m = 17.25m = 1.725 bar
  4. Estimate pump power based on required flow rate
