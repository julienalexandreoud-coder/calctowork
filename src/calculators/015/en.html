How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculate area: 10m × 3m = 30m²
  2. Apply waste: 30m² × 1.05 = 31.5m² of planchas
  3. Convert thickness: 6cm = 0.06m
  4. Calculate volume: 30m² × 0.06m = 1.8m³ of insulation
