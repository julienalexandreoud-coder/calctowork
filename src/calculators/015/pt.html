How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcular área: 10m × 3m = 30m²
  2. Aplicar quebra: 30m² × 1.05 = 31.5m² de planchas
  3. Convertir espessura: 6cm = 0.06m
  4. Calcular volume: 30m² × 0.06m = 1.8m³ de isolamento
