How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculatete daily use: 4 people x 150 L = 600 L/day
  2. Calculatete capacity: 600 L/day x 3 days = 1800 L
  3. Convert to m3: 1800 L / 1000 = 1.8 m3
  4. Add dead volume: +10% = 1980 L (commercial tank: 2000 L)
