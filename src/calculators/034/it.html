How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcolare consumo diario: 4 personas × 150 L = 600 L/día
  2. Calcolare capacidad: 600 L/día × 2 giorni = 1200 L
  3. Convertire a m³: 1200 L ÷ 1000 = 1.2 m³
  4. Considerare volume muerto: agregar 10% = 1320 L (serbatoio comercial: 1500 L)
