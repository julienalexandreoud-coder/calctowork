How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Room perimeter: 2 × (5 + 4) = 18 m
  2. Add 10% for mitre cuts and waste: 18 × 1.1 = 19.8 m
  3. Pieces of 2.4 m: ceil(19.8 ÷ 2.4) = 9 pieces

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Länge von die Raum
  • Breite von die Raum
  • Tipo von esquina
  • Länge von jede pieza

Result Outputs

  • Umfang von die Raum
  • Metros lineales erforderlich
  • Piezas notwendige
  • Piezas von esquina

Formula

Perimeter = 2 × (length + width); Linear metres needed = perimeter × 1.1; Pieces = ceil(linear_m ÷ molding_length)
