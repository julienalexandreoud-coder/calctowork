How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calcolare volume: 100m² × 2.5m = 250m³
  2. Factor isolamento medio: 40 W/m³
  3. Calcolare potenza: 250m³ × 40 W/m³ = 10000 W = 10 kW
  4. Applicare margine seguridad 20%: 10 kW × 1.20 = 12 kW
