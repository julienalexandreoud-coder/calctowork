How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Berechnen: Volumen: 100m² × 2.5m = 250m³
  2. Faktor Dämmung medio: 40 W/m³
  3. Berechnen: Leistung: 250m³ × 40 W/m³ = 10000 W = 10 kW
  4. Anwenden margen seguridad 20%: 10 kW × 1.20 = 12 kW
