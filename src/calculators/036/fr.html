How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculer volume: 100m² × 2.5m = 250m³
  2. Factor isolation medio: 40 W/m³
  3. Calculer puissance: 250m³ × 40 W/m³ = 10000 W = kW
  4. Appliquer marge seguridad 20%: kW × 1.20 = kW

Formula

Volume = surface × hauteur; puissance = volume × factor_aislamiento (30-50 W/m³)
