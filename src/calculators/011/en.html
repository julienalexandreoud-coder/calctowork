How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Calculatete gross wall area: 8m × 2.8m = 22.4m²
  2. Deduct openings: 22.4m² - 2.5m² = 19.9m² net area
  3. Calculatete bricks: 19.9m² × 32/m² × 1.05 waste = 669 bricks
  4. Calculatete mortar: 19.9m² × 8 kg/m² = 159 kg mortar

How It Works

This calculator helps you estimate the materials and quantities needed for your project.

Input Fields

  • Wall length (m)
  • Wall height (m)
  • Deduct openings (m²)

Result Outputs

  • Gross area (m²)
  • Net area (m²)
  • Hollow bricks needed
  • Mortar weight (kg)

Formula

Gross = Length x Height; Net = Gross - Openings; Bricks = Net x 32 per m²
