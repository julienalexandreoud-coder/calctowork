How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Enter a set of numbers separated by commas
  2. The mean is the sum of all values divided by the count
  3. Add all numbers together, then divide by how many there are
  4. The mean is the most common measure of central tendency
  5. It is sensitive to outliers — one extreme value can shift the mean
  6. Use median for skewed distributions
