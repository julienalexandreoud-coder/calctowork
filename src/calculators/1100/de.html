How to Use This Calculator

Follow these simple steps to get accurate results:

  1. Berechnente deck area: 5 m × 4 m = 20.00 m²
  2. Board effective width with gap: 14 cm + 0.6 cm = 14.6 cm = 0.146 m
  3. Rows across width: ceil(4 ÷ 0.146) = 28 rows
  4. Boards per row: ceil(5 ÷ 3.6) = 2 boards per row
  5. Total boards: 28 × 2 = 56 boards (58 with 5% waste)

Formula
