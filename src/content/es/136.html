Fórmula: s = √(Σ(xᵢ - x̄)² ÷ (n-1))

Ejemplo de uso: Valores separados por coma: 12.5, 15.3, 14.8, 11.2, 16.7, 13.9 .

Desglose del cálculo

  1. Dataset: 12.5, 15.3, 14.8, 11.2, 16.7, 13.9 (n=6)
  2. Calculate mean: (12.5+15.3+14.8+11.2+16.7+13.9)÷6 = 14.067
  3. Find squared deviations from mean and sum them
  4. Divide by (n-1)=5 and take square root

Errores frecuentes

  • Using population formula (÷n) instead of sample formula (÷n-1)
  • Not having enough data points (need at least 2)
  • Incorrectly parsing comma-separated input values
