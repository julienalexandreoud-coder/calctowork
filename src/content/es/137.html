Datos de entrada

  • Valores separados por coma (valor por defecto: 2,4,4,4,5,5,7,9)

Resultados

  • Varianza
  • Media

Fórmula: s² = Σ(xᵢ - x̄)² ÷ (n-1)

Ejemplo de uso: Valores separados por coma: 8.2, 10.5, 9.8, 11.3, 7.9, 10.1 .

Desglose del cálculo

  1. Dataset: 8.2, 10.5, 9.8, 11.3, 7.9, 10.1 (n=6)
  2. Calculate mean: 57.8 ÷ 6 = 9.633
  3. Find each deviation squared and sum
  4. Divide sum by (n-1)=5 to get variance

Errores frecuentes

  • Using population variance (÷n) instead of sample variance (÷n-1)
  • Confusing variance with standard deviation (variance is squared)
  • Not having minimum 2 data points
