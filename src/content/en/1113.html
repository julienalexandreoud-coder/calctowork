What is the Crown Molding Calculator?

The Crown Molding Calculator is a free online calculator that helps you compute results quickly and accurately.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your Room length.
  2. Enter your Room width.
  3. Enter your Corner type.
  4. Enter your Molding piece length.
  5. Click Calculate to see instant results.

Formula

The calculator uses this formula:

Perimeter = 2 × (length + width); Linear metres needed = perimeter × 1.1; Pieces = ceil(linear_m ÷ molding_length)

Worked Example

Using typical values (room_length = 5, room_width = 4, corner_type = 1, molding_length = 2.4):

  1. Room perimeter: 2 × (5 + 4) = 18 m
  2. Add 10% for mitre cuts and waste: 18 × 1.1 = 19.8 m
  3. Pieces of 2.4 m: ceil(19.8 ÷ 2.4) = 9 pieces

Result summary: Perimeter: {perimeter_m} m, linear metres needed: {linear_meters_needed} m, pieces: {pieces_needed}

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Not adding extra for mitre cuts — each inside corner wastes ~5 cm per piece
  • Forgetting to measure the height at which crown contacts the wall and ceiling
  • Ordering standard lengths without checking if longer pieces reduce waste
