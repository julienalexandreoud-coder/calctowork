What is the Countertop Calculator?

The Countertop Calculator is a free online calculator that helps you compute results quickly and accurately.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your Counter length.
  2. Enter your Counter depth.
  3. Enter your Backsplash height.
  4. Enter your Cutouts area.
  5. Click Calculate to see instant results.

Formula

The calculator uses this formula:

Surface area = Counter length × depth; Backsplash = length × backsplash_height; Cost = area × price/m²

Worked Example

Using typical values (counter_length = 3.5, counter_depth = 0.6, backsplash_height = 0.45, cutouts = 1):

  1. Counter surface: 3.5 m × 0.6 m = 2.1 m²
  2. Backsplash area: 3.5 m × 0.45 m = 1.575 m²
  3. Subtract cutout allowance: 1 sink cutout ≈ 0.2 m²
  4. Total slab needed: 2.1 + 1.575 - 0.2 = 3.475 m²

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Forgetting to account for overhangs (typically 3-4 cm on seating sides)
  • Not measuring the backsplash area separately from the counter
  • Assuming one slab covers everything — large kitchens often need seams
