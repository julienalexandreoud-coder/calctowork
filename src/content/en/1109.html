What is the Backsplash Tile Calculator?

The Backsplash Tile Calculator is a free online calculator that helps you compute results quickly and accurately.

How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter your Wall length.
  2. Enter your Wall height.
  3. Enter your Tile size.
  4. Enter your Window/door openings.
  5. Click Calculate to see instant results.

Formula

The calculator uses this formula:

Area = (Wall length × height) - window openings; Tiles = ceil(area ÷ tile_size² × 1.1); Boxes = ceil(tiles ÷ per_box)

Worked Example

Using typical values (wall_length = 3, wall_height = 0.6, tile_size = 10, window_openings = 0):

  1. Backsplash area: 3 m × 0.6 m = 1.8 m²
  2. 10 cm tile area: 0.10 × 0.10 = 0.01 m² per tile
  3. Tiles needed: ceil(1.8 ÷ 0.01 × 1.1) = 198 tiles
  4. Typical box = 25 tiles → 8 boxes

Result summary: Wall area: {wall_area_m2} m², tiles needed: {tiles_needed}, boxes: {boxes_needed}, waste: {waste_percent}%

Common Mistakes to Avoid

  • Measuring tile size in centimetres but area in metres — must be consistent
  • Forgetting the grout gap when placing tiles (use actual coverage area)
  • Not adding 10% extra for cuts, especially on mosaic or herringbone patterns
